Introduction

iClassic Digital respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in our practices.

Information We Collect

Core Commitment:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • All data is stored locally
  • No usage tracking
  • Photos never uploaded to servers

iClassic Digital does not collect any personal information. All app preferences and unlock statuses are stored locally on your device. We do not track your usage or collect analytics data. Photos you process remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no data usage to report. Your collection progress and preferences exist solely on your device to enhance your experience with the app.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and local storage. This includes your unlock progress, preferences, and settings. No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

Completely Independent:

  • No third-party analytics
  • No advertising services
  • No tracking services
  • Fully offline operation

iClassic Digital does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline and does not require network access for core features.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or resetting the app data in iOS Settings. We do not have access to any of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13, as we don't collect any personal information from anyone. Parents can safely allow children to use this app.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in app updates. We will notify users of significant changes through the App Store update notes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please feel free to contact us through the App Store review section or by email. We value your feedback and concerns.
